Executive interpretation
A summary connecting the data to strategy.

What changed in 2025

Regional performance became more uneven. Asia ex Japan, Japan, and the United States all showed softer revenue, while Europe remained relatively stable. At the same time, store expansion continued despite declining revenue and profit per store.

What it means

LVMH is facing two interconnected challenges: declining store-level efficiency internally, and increasing regional demand volatility externally. This indicates that growth is becoming less predictable and less efficient.

How to solve

LVMH should adopt a more data-driven approach to expansion decisions by evaluating store efficiency, local customer behavior, regional performance, and demand patterns. This enables more selective and effective expansion planning, ensuring that growth improves both performance and resilience. The goal is to support creativity with better decisions, not replace human design judgment.
